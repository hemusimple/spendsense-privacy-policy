# SpendSense — Privacy Policy

**Last updated: May 2026**  
**Developer: Hemanth**  
**App: SpendSense — Personal Finance Tracker**

---

## 1. Overview
SpendSense is a personal finance tracking application designed to work 
completely offline. This Privacy Policy explains how SpendSense handles 
your information.

---

## 2. Information We Do NOT Collect
SpendSense does **not** collect, process, store, or transmit:
- Personal identification information (name, email, phone number)
- Financial account details or bank information
- Location data
- Device identifiers
- Usage analytics or crash reports
- Advertising identifiers
- Any data to external servers, cloud services, or third parties

---

## 3. Data Stored Locally on Your Device
All data you enter in SpendSense is stored **only on your device**:
- Expense records and categories
- Budget and savings goals
- Bill photos and receipts
- Group expense data
- Debt records
- Recurring expense settings

This data never leaves your device unless you explicitly choose 
to export or share it yourself.

---

## 4. Internet and Network Access
SpendSense does **not** require an internet connection.  
SpendSense does **not** make any network requests.  
SpendSense does **not** connect to any servers.

---

## 5. Backup Data
When you use the backup feature, SpendSense creates a ZIP file saved 
to the **Downloads/SpendSense/** folder on your device's local storage.
This backup file:
- Stays on your device only
- Is never uploaded anywhere automatically
- Is your responsibility to manage and secure

If you manually share this backup file via WhatsApp, email, Google Drive 
or any other service, that sharing is entirely under your control and 
subject to the privacy policies of those services.

---

## 6. Permissions
SpendSense requests only the permissions necessary for its features:

| Permission | Purpose |
|---|---|
| Camera | Capture bill/receipt photos |
| Read Media Images | Pick photos from gallery |
| Post Notifications | Recurring expense reminders |
| Write External Storage | Save backup on Android 9 and below only |

> **Note:** Additional permissions may be requested in future versions 
> if new features are introduced. This policy will be updated 
> accordingly before any such release.

No permission is used for data collection or tracking.

---

## 7. Third-Party Services
SpendSense currently does **not** integrate any third-party services.

However, future versions of SpendSense may include:
- **Advertisements** — served by third-party ad networks such as 
  Google AdMob. If ads are introduced, this policy will be updated 
  to reflect what data the ad network may collect.
- **In-app purchases or subscriptions** — processed through 
  Google Play Billing. Google Play's own privacy policy will apply 
  to any payment transactions. SpendSense itself will never directly 
  collect or store your payment or card details.

We will notify users of any such changes by updating this Privacy 
Policy and incrementing the "Last updated" date at the top of this page.

Currently active third-party integrations: **None**

---

## 8. Children's Privacy
SpendSense does not knowingly collect any information from anyone, 
including children under the age of 13. Since no data is collected 
at all, SpendSense is safe for all ages.

---

## 9. Data Security
Since all data is stored locally on your device, the security of 
your data depends on your device security (screen lock, encryption, etc.). 
We recommend keeping your device secured and regularly backing up 
your data using the built-in backup feature.

---

## 10. Data Deletion
To permanently delete all your SpendSense data:

1. **App data** — Uninstall SpendSense from your device. This 
   permanently removes all records, settings, and photos stored 
   by the app.

2. **Backup files** — Open your device's file manager, navigate to 
   **Downloads/SpendSense/** and delete the folder manually.

Since we never receive your data, we have nothing to delete on our end.

---

## 11. Changes to This Policy
We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will 
be posted on this page with an updated date. We encourage you to review 
this page periodically. Continued use of the app after changes 
constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

---

## 12. Your Rights
Since SpendSense collects no personal data, there is no data for us 
to provide, correct, or delete on our end. You have full control over 
all your data at all times through your device.

advice. Always consult a qualified professional for financial decisions.
